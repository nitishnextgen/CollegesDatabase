UG Admission Guide

Stream-wise admission requirements, entrance exams, documents needed, and top college recommendations

How to Use This Guide

This comprehensive guide covers 8 major academic streams with detailed information about entrance exams, qualifications, required documents, and top universities. Click on any stream card below to expand and view complete details.

Engineering

Entrance Exams

JEE Main/JEE Advanced (India), SAT/ACT (US), GATE (PG), GRE (International PG)

Qualifications

High school diploma with strong marks in Mathematics & Physics. Some universities require minimum 75% in PCM subjects.

Required Documents

Application form, 10th/12th mark sheets, diploma (if any), passport-size photo, ID proof, SOP (for PG), recommendation letters

Top Colleges

IIT Delhi IIT Bombay MIT Stanford ETH Zurich TUM

Computer Science

Entrance Exams

JEE Main/Advanced, SAT/ACT, university-specific tests; GATE (PG), GRE (International)

Qualifications

High school diploma with Mathematics. Programming knowledge and personal projects are highly valued.

Required Documents

Transcripts, CV/Resume, portfolio/projects (GitHub), recommendation letters (PG), Statement of Purpose

Top Colleges

MIT Stanford IITs NUS Tsinghua CMU

Business / Management

Entrance Exams

CAT/XAT (India MBA), GMAT/GRE (International PG), university-specific UG tests, SAT for US universities

Qualifications

High school diploma for UG. For MBA: Bachelor's degree + 2-5 years work experience preferred.

Required Documents

Transcripts, CV/Resume, Statement of Purpose, Letters of Recommendation, entrance test scores, work experience certificates (MBA)

Top Colleges

IIM Ahmedabad Wharton Stanford GSB LBS INSEAD

Medicine (MBBS)

Entrance Exams

NEET-UG (India - mandatory), UCAT/BMAT (UK), MCAT (US/Canada), university-specific tests abroad

Qualifications

High school diploma with Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. Minimum 50-60% in PCB required for NEET eligibility.

Required Documents

Mark sheets, NEET/entrance score card, medical fitness certificate, character certificate, background verification, caste certificate (if applicable)

Top Colleges

AIIMS Delhi CMC Vellore U of Toronto UCL Karolinska

Law

Entrance Exams

CLAT/AILET (India UG), LNAT (UK), LSAT (US/International), university-specific aptitude tests

Qualifications

High school diploma for 5-year integrated LLB. For 3-year LLB: Bachelor's degree in any discipline.

Required Documents

Transcripts, entrance test score, Statement of Purpose, recommendation letters, legal internship certificates (if any)

Top Colleges

NLSIU Bangalore NALSAR Oxford Harvard Law LSE

Natural & Life Sciences

Entrance Exams

University entrance exams, national tests (CUET in India), SAT Subject Tests, specific science aptitude tests

Qualifications

Strong science background in high school (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics). Research experience valued.

Required Documents

Transcripts, research/project summaries, Statement of Purpose (PG), recommendation letters from teachers

Top Colleges

Cambridge Oxford Tsinghua UChicago IISc

Architecture

Entrance Exams

NATA/JEE Paper 2 (India), portfolio review (international), CEED (PG), university-specific studio tests

Qualifications

High school diploma with Mathematics. Strong aptitude for design, drawing, and spatial visualization required.

Required Documents

Portfolio (10-20 creative works), transcripts, entrance test scores, Statement of Purpose, sketches/drawings

Top Colleges

MIT UCL Bartlett Politecnico Milano SPA Delhi NID

Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences

Entrance Exams

Often merit-based or CUET (India), university-specific essays, SAT/ACT for US, aptitude tests for specific programs

Qualifications

High school diploma with relevant subject background. Strong writing, analytical, and critical thinking skills valued.

Required Documents

Transcripts, writing samples/essays, recommendation letters, Statement of Purpose (PG), creative portfolio (for arts)

Top Colleges

Oxford Cambridge LSE UChicago JNU

Pro Tips for Successful Admission

1

Start Early

Begin preparation at least 1-2 years before application deadlines. Research universities and their requirements thoroughly.

2

Build Your Profile

Focus on academics, extracurriculars, internships, and projects. A well-rounded profile stands out in competitive admissions.

3

Prepare Strong Documents

Craft a compelling SOP, secure strong recommendation letters, and maintain organized documentation of all achievements.

4

Apply Strategically

Apply to a mix of dream, target, and safety universities. Keep track of deadlines and scholarship opportunities.
